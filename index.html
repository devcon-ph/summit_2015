Speakers

"The more that you learn, the more places you'll go." - Dr. Seuss

Eric Su

Eric Su is the CEO of a modern social network called Lifebit - a social network around life goals and happiness and City Lifebit - a photo for cash app for iOS and Android.

Involved in the technical decision as well as product design - Eric knows the product development process intimately and is always an open book about design and entrepreneurship.

Joshua Lat

Joshua Lat serves as the Scrum Master, Lead Developer, and Infrastructure Engineer of Deal Grocer, Taste Central, and BeautyMNL. In the past, he's known for winning the Kaspersky International Cup 2012 which made him interested in IT Security and E-commerce. For the past couple of years, he has been sharing his knowledge and experiences in several conferences and meetups around the country to help spread awareness and discuss working solutions to common problems in startups and web development.

Phil Smithson

Phil Smithson is a User Experience expert with over 10 years experience in the digital landscape. He passionately believes in putting users first in everything he does and his main goal in life is to make users lives easier through excellent Service Design. He is the co-founder of UXMNL and a director of On-Off Design & Technology with offices in Hong Kong, Manila and Melbourne.

Wilson Censon

Wilson is a Co-Founder and Chief Marketing Officer of Mobility Creative Labs, a mobile and game studio based in Nueva Ecija. He is also an Assistant Lead of Google Educators Group Nueva Ecija, a GEG Philippines chapter who trains teachers in K-12 technology. He also functions as an External Affairs and a Part-Time IT Instructor in Asian Institute of E-Commerce Gapan Campus. Currently, Wilson is also the Marketing Director in EDGEKIT Computer System, located in Taguig City.

Andre Marcelo-Tanner

Andre Marcelo-Tanner is the Director of Technology for Examine.com

He has been developing and tinkering with websites since 1997. Understanding how important it was to make information accessible, he has a deep understanding on how to present information to a user that is both easy to use but also brings a depth of data.

In his free time, Andre likes to run, and sleep.

Neil Patrick Del Gallego

A game developer for 4 years at Playlab Inc. (formerly Anino Games), Neil has released projects from partnered clients and IP projects, namely, GraveMania for Android, Cherry Dots and Sago Express.

Newly released match-3 mobile games like Dragon Cubes and Bubble Cubes, Neil is assigned to tackle the UI system and ensure that assets used in the game are optimised.

Neil is also currently taking his Masters Degree in Computer Science at De La Salle University. He is also interested in pursuing high-level research in the computing field.

Marcieb Balisacan

Marcieb Balisacan is an independent game developer, designer, and producer who has worked on games from mobile, casual, online multiplayer to AAA titles. With a background in computer science and multimedia, and more than 13 years experience in the games industry, he has worked as a producer, game designer, technical lead, and studio manager. He has been a technical reviewer in publications from Packt Publishing covering game design and development topics using CryEngine, Unreal, and Unity. He has co-founded a game development studio startup named Full Mana Studios, and currently holds the title of Lead Game Engineer at Synergy88 Digital.

Christopher David

Toph is a VR Evangelist and Developer. He works as an R&D Engineer at Nokia by day but leads a double life in the metaverse. He co-founded VR Philippines to boost the local Virtual Reality community and help put the Philippines in the forefront of this exciting technological revolution. He is currently setting up his own VR startup, Veer, which aims to bring virtual reality to everyone. He also serves as the Board Member for VR at ACM SIGGRAPH Manila.

Michelle Anne Tabirao

Michelle is a core member of Raspberry Pi Philippines and a Computer Engineer. She is an IOT enthusiast and a Program Coordinator/Open Source Evangelist of 8layer Technologies Inc. focused on collaborative frameworks for academe, government, community, media, and corporate partners through open source technology and hackathon initiatives.

Christian Angelo Labao

Ian Labao is a web geek, enthusiast and developer from PH. He does seminars in different universities in the country to promote local IT industry to acadame and encourage students to be part of the fun and exciting IT industry in our country.

Lonel Labit

Lonel is the Country Manager for HERE Philippines and manages the Regional Map and Content Operations in the Country. He is an experienced IT executive with over 11 years of industry experience working with different multi-national companies like Dubai Ports World, ABS-CBN, and Nokia. He took up BSBA Computer Management at the Colegio de San Juan de Letran Manila. He is a software guy by heart and also loves travelling. He loves maps not just as part of his profession and he believes maps can help us navigate and solve complex problems and built effective solutions using it. He love maps; that’s why he is here.

Luis Caezar Ian Panganiban

Ian is a Science Research Specialist II for a DOST-PCIEERD funded project which deals with a development of Web-based Geographic Information Systems for tidal current energy. He is a licensed geodetic engineer and a part time WebGIS consultant/developer. He enjoys hackathons, 'chilling out' and reading about technology, mapping and anything about geo.

Gian Crescini Santillan

Gian has been a PHP developer for 5 years, won 2 hackathons, spoke in various events. A PHP Freak.

Prem Vilas Fortran Rara

Prem is an Engineer (and partner) at Cascadeo PH. Likes to be in the sea and spend quite weekends with broadsheets and GitHub.

Jan Michael Ibanez

JM is a software jack-of-all-trades; he's interested in the whole spectrum of software from UI and UX, to the nitty gritty of backends. His day job involves building backend systems for clients and architecting his company's core product, an ERP system for small businesses. In his spare time, he dabbles with photograpy, lettering, and sketching.

Ayo Ayco

Ayo is currently the Project Manager for WebSAFE at Project NOAH, funded by UNICEF and the DOST. A proud Iskolar ng Bayan, he worked at UP Los Baños as a System Analyst before moving back to his hometown in Quezon City to work with Project NOAH. As he leads the team of developers and GIS specialists for WebSAFE, he also engages entrepreneurs and leaders for a better Web as founder of AbsorbingDesign.com. He loves to play guitar, write blogs, and eat Adobo. Ayo is also a proud babywearing dad to his 3-month old son, Kahel.

Alvine Garcia Aurelio

Alvine is a Software Engineer at Accenture/Avanade PDC. He is a Microsoft Student Partner Alumnus and currently a Microsoft Technology Evangelist where he is a Speaker/Trainer in different Microsoft Communities. A PHINUG Speaker for the Windows 10 App Dev, he is also a Microsoft Technology Associate in Software Development, DBA, Web Dev, Networking etc. and Microsoft Certified Professional. He also joined Microsoft Imagine Cup 2015 and became one of the National Finalists. He has also competed and won in various hackathons in the past few years.

Aivy Torres

Aivy is a Senior Principal Software Architect at Infor. He has been in the IT industry for almost 15 years, and specializes on mobile application development. He has done a number of talks regarding mobile trends and mobility enterprise applications within and outside the country, and is also a proponent of beautiful software. He was a finalist in the Young Leader of the Year Award by the Asia CEO body last 2013, and winner of the Manager of the Year awarded by Infor on the same year.

Anthony Escalona

Anthony is an Engineering Manager in Test at Medidata Solutions. He plays an integral role in advocating code quality by providing extensive system and functional level tests. For big data projects, he was able to create a comprehensive CI environment with automated tests, code analyses, validation, and deployment builds that were integral components in allowing several projects to be high quality in their functionality and their deployments. This has created a tight feedback loop where problems are found and resolved quickly. In his spare time, Anthony loves to bowl and play foosball.
